About Me

I'm Madeleine, it's nice to meet you!

I am a sophomore in the School of Information studying UX Design. I am originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, but I love Ann Arbor and all it has to offer. As of now, I hope to graduate in 3 years, achieve a 1-year Masters in Information, and then pursue a career in Product Design or Product Management. I would also love to continue my love for fitness in some way, possibly by becoming a part-time fitness instructor.

My Fitness Journey

In high school, I swam varsity and played club volleyball. Here at Michigan, I try to uphold a healthy lifestyle. This means eating a balanced diet and exercising 6-7 times/week. My favorite workouts are SoulCycle, swimming, HIIT, and hiking. I compete on the women’s club water polo team, which has been such a great community to be a part of, and I also work for UM Recreational Sports as a lifeguard. I look forward to seeing what I can achieve in the future for both my physical and mental wellbeing.

Summer 2021 Goals

In order to maintain my physical health, I’ve set a series of fitness goals for myself to achieve this summer. It won’t be easy, but I look forward to pushing myself to new limits while trying new things. Here are my goals:

  • Get my personal training certification 🏋
  • Complete my first sprint triathlon 🏅
  • Complete my first half marathon 🏃
  • Reach 50 SoulCycle rides 🚲
