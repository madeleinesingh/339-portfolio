Exercise

Exercise improves mental health by reducing anxiety, depression, and negative mood and by improving self-esteem and cognitive function. Exercise has also been found to alleviate symptoms such as low self-esteem and social withdrawal.

Getting consistent exercise is the best way to get fit, so I recommend working out at least three or four times a week. Not sure where to start? Don’t worry - on this page, I’ve included all of my favorite local activities that are sure to get your heart pumping!


Local Studios

After living in Ann Arbor for a couple of years, I have looked near and far for fun, new workouts in local studios. Although classes typically cost money, most of these studios have amazing new member discounts, so they’re definitely worth a try if you’re looking to go out and try something new. For those of you outside of AA, have no fear: most of these studios have multiple locations. Here are some of my all-time favorites:

SoulCycle

  • Cycling classes accompanied by EDM music
  • 1107 S University Ave, Ann Arbor, MI

MVMNT Fit

  • Offers heart-pumping cycling, HIIT, and strength classes
  • 1300 S University Ave, Ann Arbor, MI

Barre Code

  • Offers HIIT classes in three different formats: Barre, Bootcamp, Brawl
  • 2793 Plymouth Rd, Ann Arbor, MI

City Row

  • Rowing classes that incorporate strength training
  • 401 E Liberty St, Ann Arbor, MI

Red Yoga

  • Offers a variety of yoga classes such as "hot yoga"
  • 421 E Liberty St, Ann Arbor, MI

SolidCore

  • Unique pilates workout designed to burn fat and build muscle
  • 3081 Oak Valley Dr, Ann Arbor, MI

Free Fun

For those of us who enjoy being outside, who are balling on a budget, or who may not be confident taking a workout class with other people, I’ve taken note of my favorite free activities in Ann Arbor. These are great to do alone or with friends, and most are available any time of the year … weather permitting!

UM Rec Sports

  • Facilities that are free for student use
  • Multiple locations on campus

Nichols Arboretum

  • Beautiful trails surrounded by greenery
  • 1610 Washington Heights, Ann Arbor

Palmer Field Track

  • Great for running or just spending time outside
  • 401 Washtenaw Ave, Ann Arbor
